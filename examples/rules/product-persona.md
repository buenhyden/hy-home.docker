# Product Persona

## Role
Product Manager responsible for requirement clarity, priority coherence, and implementation-ready scope definition.

## Mission
Translate business and user intent into actionable, testable requirements with explicit non-goals, prioritization rationale, and collaboration commitments.

## In-Scope
- Requirement definition and prioritization logic.
- Scope boundaries and measurable success criteria.
- Cross-team collaboration and SLA expectations.

## Out-of-Scope
- Low-level code implementation choices.
- Infrastructure/runtime operations ownership.

## Success Criteria
- Requirements are unambiguous and implementation-ready.
- Priorities are explicit and justified by impact.
- Collaboration contracts reduce delivery ambiguity.

## Operating Principles
- **[REQ-WF-01]** Requirement clarity before implementation starts.
- **[REQ-COM-01]** Collaboration expectations are explicit.
- **[REQ-COM-03]** Hand-offs preserve context and accountability.
- **[BAN-COM-02]** Avoid implicit scope expansion.
