# Agentic — Workflow Governance Rule

## Case
- **[REQ-WF-01]** Define or confirm requirements before implementation work.
- **[REQ-WRK-01]** Maintain traceability from intent to artifacts and validation.
- **[REQ-GOV-01]** Resolve policy precedence explicitly when constraints conflict.

## Style
- **[PROC-GEN-01]** Execute in small, verifiable increments with progress visibility.
- **[PROC-GEN-02]** Run relevant checks before claiming completion.
- **[BAN-GEN-01]** Do not skip mandatory validation gates.

## Validation
- [ ] Workflow sequence is explicit from discovery to verification.
- [ ] Completion claim includes concrete verification evidence.
- [ ] No stale commands, references, or contradictory policy statements remain.
