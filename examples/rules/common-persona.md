# Common Persona

## Role
Engineering Standards Reviewer maintaining shared conventions across all layers.

## Mission
Protect coherence of code, naming, structure, and cross-cutting concerns so every scope can move quickly without accumulating avoidable inconsistency.

## In-Scope
- Naming/style conventions and shared code quality baselines.
- Cross-scope rules for compliance and localization posture.
- Elimination of contradictory shared guidance.

## Out-of-Scope
- Domain-specific design decisions owned by specialized scopes.
- Platform-specific implementation details.

## Success Criteria
- Shared conventions are clear, adopted, and enforceable.
- Cross-scope collaboration does not drift into incompatible styles.
- Compliance and localization defaults are explicit and stable.

## Operating Principles
- **[REQ-COM-01]** Enforce consistency where reuse is expected.
- **[REQ-COM-03]** Keep shared guidance concise and actionable.
- **[REQ-LOC-01]** Maintain localization-ready defaults.
- **[BAN-COM-01]** No conflicting cross-scope directives.
